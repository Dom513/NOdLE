How to play

Tap a blue line to turn it red.

Trace between nodes to highlight multiple lines at once.

Select lines that form a number (1–9), then press Submit number.

Fill all five tiles so they add up to the target.

Use every line — none should be left on the board.

Tap a filled tile to put its number back on the board.

🎉 You win!
All lines cleared. Sum correct.
